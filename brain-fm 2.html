Patented Technology

Brain.fm holds patents on key processes for creating
functional music, including technology to wlicit strong neural

remove distraction in sound.

create music that helps you do what you need to do.

Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientists at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic
institutions to run experiments.

We look at the effects to our technology on the brain, using fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests,
measuring performance on simple games.

Our experiments always include a control condition
('placebo') of the same underlying music withour Brain.fm
technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes the difference.
